Dubrovnik’s hidden world

Under the photographs is a layer most visitors never reach, the one the people here would give their lives for.

The face of season one is Ruđer Bošković, the scientist and diplomat born inside those walls. A small republic outlasted the empires around it not by matching their armies, but by being cleverer than all of them.
